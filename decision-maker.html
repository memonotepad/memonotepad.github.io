How to Use

Four Ways to Make a Decision

📋 Pick from List

Enter your options (one per line) and click "Pick Random". The tool randomly selects one item instantly.

🎡 Spin Wheel

Visual wheel spinner with colorful segments. Click spin and watch the wheel rotate before landing on a choice.

🪙 Coin Flip

Classic heads or tails. The coin flips with animation and lands on a random side.

✅ Yes / No

Ask any question and get a random yes or no answer. Perfect for quick binary decisions.
