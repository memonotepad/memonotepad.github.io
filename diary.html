How to Use

Writing in Your Online Diary

Three simple steps to start journaling privately and consistently.

📅 Pick a Date

The diary automatically shows today's date. You can also select a past date to add or edit an entry retroactively.

✍️ Write Freely

Add a title and pour your thoughts into the editor. Choose a mood to capture how you felt. Everything stays private.

💾 Auto-Save & Reflect

Your entry saves automatically. Come back anytime to read past entries, edit them, or export your journal.

Why use an online diary? This digital journal is perfect for daily reflection, gratitude practice, mental health check-ins, or simply preserving memories. Because it's stored locally in your browser, nobody else can access your entries — not even us.
