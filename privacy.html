Privacy Policy

Your privacy is at the core of Memo Notepad. We believe your thoughts should remain yours alone.

1. Data Storage (Local-First)

Memo Notepad is a "local-first" web application. All memos you create are stored exclusively in your browser's localStorage. We do not maintain any database or cloud storage for your notes.

  • Your memos never leave your device.
  • We cannot read, access, or recover your memos.
  • Deleting your browser cache or history may delete your memos unless you have exported them.

2. Information We Collect

Because there is no account registration, we do not collect personal information such as names, email addresses, or phone numbers. Our website may use basic, anonymized analytical tools to understand general traffic patterns (e.g., page views), but this data is not linked to your individual memos.

3. Third-Party Services

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your data to outside parties. Since your data remains on your machine, there is no data to transfer.

4. Security

By keeping data on your local device, we eliminate the risk of server-side data breaches. However, the security of your memos depends on the security of your device and browser. We recommend exporting your memos as .txt or .json files for extra backup.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
