How to Use

Shuffle Text in Different Ways

Flexible shuffling modes for any kind of text.

📝 Shuffle Words

Randomly reorders the words in your text while keeping sentence structure. Perfect for poetry or creative prompts.

📄 Shuffle Sentences

Reorders entire sentences randomly. Useful for restructuring paragraphs or generating alternative flows.

🔤 Shuffle Letters

Randomizes the order of letters within each word. Creates anagram-style puzzles or obfuscated text.

📑 Shuffle Paragraphs

Reorders paragraphs (separated by blank lines) to create new document structures instantly.
